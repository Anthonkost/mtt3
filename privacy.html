MEGA TECHNO TRON 3000 — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2025

We are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Users located in the United States are entitled to similar rights under certain state laws. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address (when submitted via our form powered by Formspree)
  • Description of your business process (submitted via the same form)
  • Your email address and optional details when booking a call via Cal.com

We do not use tracking cookies, or any behavioral advertising tools.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To generate a custom automation report based on your input
  • To contact you if you book a call via Cal.com

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

3. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent when submitting a form or booking a call.

4. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at root@mtt3000.com.

6. Third-Party Tools

We use the following external services:

  • Formspree – to securely collect submitted form data
  • Cal.com – to schedule calls with user consent

These services are GDPR-compliant and process data under their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

For any questions regarding this policy, email us at root@mtt3000.com.

8. Analytics

We use a session analytics tool called Smartlook to better understand how users interact with our website. Smartlook collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, clicks, scroll depth, and device type.

This data helps us improve our website and service offering. Smartlook may use cookies to operate. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored by Smartlook unless explicitly provided by you through a form.

For more information, visit Smartlook's Privacy Policy.
